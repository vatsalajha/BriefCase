NON-COMPETITION AGREEMENT

The Employee agrees that during the term of employment and for a period of
twenty-four (24) months following the termination of employment for any reason,
the Employee shall not, directly or indirectly, engage in, own, manage, operate,
control, be employed by, participate in, or be connected with any business that
competes with the Company's business within a seventy-five (75) mile radius of
any office location where the Employee was assigned during the last two (2) years
of employment.

This restriction applies to all states where the Company operates, including
California, Texas, New York, Florida, and Delaware.

In the event any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the
court shall modify such provision to the minimum extent necessary to make it
enforceable, rather than voiding it entirely.
